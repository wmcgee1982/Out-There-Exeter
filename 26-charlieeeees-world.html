Event Description

charlieeeee's World: Live at RAMM


Step into an evening of live and acoustic sounds with charlieeeee, supported by Stevie Nicole Brown. A uniquely intimate and interactive night and a perfect setting for charlieeeee and Stevie to connect with their fans on a personal level.


charlieeeee has made a name as a go-to collaborator for artists like Fred again.., RAYE, MNEK, piri & tommy, venbee, and girli, among others.


charlieeeee's sound defies easy categorisation, blending elements of indie with the energy of jungle breakbeats. charlieeeee is an infectious, hyper-pop inspired and playful delight.


Supporting charlieeeee is the mesmerising pop performer Stevie Nicole Brown, a rising star in the UK pop scene. Stevie expertly captures the essence of modern pop music, blending elements of pop, indie, and techno to create a sound that is both nostalgic and refreshingly new.


Don't miss this chance to experience charlieeeee and Stevie Nicole Brown live, for an unforgettable and unique intimate night of music and fun.
