Event Description

Shelf used to joke about being mistaken for teenage boys. Now, it's time to grow up. It's time to stop being teenage boys and start being teenage men.


This new hour from the 'easy to fall in love with' (★★★★ Scotsman) double-act features anecdotes, songs, jokes about performing for kids, the Instagram algorithm, confronting your toxicity, and more.
