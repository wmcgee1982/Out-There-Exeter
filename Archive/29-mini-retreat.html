From Light to Dark Mini Retreat

Sun, 06 Oct 2024 | Royal Albert Memorial Museum (RAMM)

Event Details

Date: Sun, 06 Oct 2024

Time: 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Running Time: 2 hours

Venue: Royal Albert memorial Museum Art & Gallery, Queen Street, Exeter,EX4 3RX

Genre: Wellbeing, Retreat, Health

Event Description

Out There Queer Fest: From Light to Dark Mini Retreat


As Out There Queer Fest comes to a close after over two weeks of an incredible lineup featuring more than 30 events, we invite you to join us for a relaxing and rejuvenating experience at our "From Light to Dark Mini Retreat." This workshop is designed to help you transition into the autumn and winter months with grace and ease.


Through a series of yoga, meditation, and relaxation practices, you'll learn techniques to manage the stresses of everyday life and societal challenges. This retreat offers a safe and welcoming space for LGBTQ+ individuals and allies to unwind, refocus, and find inner peace as the seasons change. Come relax, recharge, and connect with your community in a supportive environment.
