Exeter Contemporary Open 2024

DAILY from 13/09| Exeter Phoenix

Event Details

Date: DAILY

Venue: Exeter Phoenix, Gandy Street, Exeter, EX4 3LS

Genre: Art, Art and Exhibitionns, Gallery

Event Description

Exeter Contemporary Open is an annual exhibition, selected from hundreds of entries, submitted by artists from across the UK. It forms a major highlight of the South West's cultural calendar and a vital springboard for the careers of emerging contemporary artists.


This year's shortlist showcases the work of fifteen contemporary visual artists who work across painting, sculpture, photography and video. Out There Queer Fest are proud to highlight three of the artists:


SHARP

Artworks in photography, sculpture and video that stem from an archive of imagery that dates back to the 1980's, to document their life as a queer artist, activist and producer.


Jess Scott

Filmmaker and photographer presenting self-portrait images from the series 'My Father's Daughter' using their father's camera, wearing his clothes and cropping their long hair to look like him.


Ruth Calland

Paintings that explore gender and gender identity based on images produced by and about trans and non-binary people, often using Tiktok to find source material.
