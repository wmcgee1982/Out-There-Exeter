Izzy Available Instagram


Event Description

Get Ready to Be Dragged into a Night of Naughty Fun!


For one night only, Exeter's campiest queens are ready to drag you out of the ordinary and into a world of extraordinary and outrageous fun! Hosted by the deliciously daring Izzy Available, this is your invitation to a scandalously good time, featuring jaw-dropping performances from Diesel, Darcii Delore, and Cleo Toris.


Expect the unexpected as our queens serve up a cocktail of sass, sparkle, and just the right amount of sin. From eye-popping dance routines to sultry singing and lip-syncs that will leave you breathless, this event is bursting with mischief, love, and high-energy entertainment that'll drag you to the edge of your seat!


Drag Me Out is more than just a show—it's an experience and a representation of the communities' local artists. We just want to make people smile and laugh while representing the community and making people feel welcome and loved.


These queens are here to push your boundaries, break societies' rules, and drag you right into the thick of the action. You're in for a night of wild, untamed drag that'll leave you begging for more — see you there, darling!
