Out There Queer Fest

Calling LGBTQIA+ folks and allies! Get ready for a vibrant explosion of creativity, community, and celebration at Out There Queer Fest!

This exciting 14-day festival happening from September 23rd to October 6th, 2024, promises an unforgettable experience.

Celebrate Culture

Out There Exeter, the driving force behind this event, is dedicated to uplifting and celebrating the city's vibrant LGBTQIA+ community.

Out There Queer Fest takes this mission a step further, creating a platform for arts, culture, sports, and advocacy.

Expect a dynamic programme featuring creative expression through artistic performances, cultural events showcasing the community's vibrancy, and sporting activities that go beyond competition, fostering a spirit of inclusion and empowerment. The festival also highlights advocacy efforts, providing a platform to champion equality and challenge discrimination.


Big Dreams

Out There Exeter envisions Out There Queer Fest becoming a national annual event, transforming Exeter into a cultural hub akin to the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.

This year's event lays the foundation, strategically centering the LGBTQIA+ community and collaborating with established venues like Exeter Phoenix, RAMM, Exeter Library, and Exeter Barnfield Theatre.
