Out There Queer Fest

WE HAVE ARRIVED! Listen-up LGBTQIA+ folks and allies! Get ready for a vibrant explosion of creativity, community, and celebration at Out There Queer Fest! This exciting 14-day festival happening from September 23rd to October 6th, 2024, promises an unforgettable experience.

Celebrate Culture

Out There Exeter, the organisation behind this event, is dedicated to uplifting and celebrating the vibrancy of the LGBTQIA+ community. Out There Queer Fest is a step further: it's a platform for arts, culture, sports, and advocacy.

Expect a dynamic programme featuring creative expression through artistic performances, cultural events, and sporting activities; fostering a spirit of inclusion and empowerment. The festival also highlights advocacy efforts, providing a platform to champion equality and challenge discrimination.

Big Dreams

Out There Exeter envisions Out There Queer Fest becoming a national annual event, transforming Exeter into a cultural hub akin to the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.

This year's event lays the foundation, strategically centering the festival around the LGBTQIA+ community and collaborating with Exeter's flagship cultural venues: Exeter Phoenix, Exeter Northcott Theatre, Barnfield Theatre, Royal Albert Memorial Museum, and Exeter Library.
