About the Event

In association with Excel–Exeter, and in collaboration with Exeter Pride, Out There Exeter, Teignbridge Community Project, Safebreaks Devon CIC, and Sing and Sign Jukebox.

Join us for two and half hours of activities and performances in which there is a safe and inclusive space where LGBTQ+ people with disabilities and neurodivergence can come together, be themselves, and have fun—whether that means getting involved or simply enjoying the atmosphere.

Access All Aerials will be there to capture the activities and event with photos and videos, and you might even get the chance to be interviewed for their radio show!

Reach Out works to break down barriers, challenge misconceptions, and reshape outdated views by ensuring greater representation of LGBTQ+ people with learning and physical disabilities, and neurodivergence. We want to empower people with disabilities and neurodivergence to participate in social activities and share experiences.

Activities

From 2.30pm, there will be a mix of activities to enjoy in a relaxed and welcoming space. You can take part in creative workshops, watch and learn from others, or do your own thing at your own pace.

Disco and Photo Corner with Excel-Exeter and Alisha B

Join in the fun with our resident drag performer, Alisha B and Excel-Exeter! Enjoy photo props, sensory lighting, and request your favourite songs to play.

- Excel-Exeter   - Alisha B

Group Visual Art Creation with Teignbridge Community Project

A chicken wire frame, with lots of different materials that guests can attach to the chicken wire with messages or using different techniques to create a united piece of artwork.

Experience Collage with Safebreaks Devon CIC

Join Safebreaks Devon CIC to create a visual community collage, sharing your stories with the help of prompt cards to inspire conversation.

Pride Makeup by Mags

Mags will be offering free sparkle and glitter makeup for anyone who wants to shine and dazzle for Pride!


Mini Masterpieces with Storm in a Teacup

Create a free, small collage to take home and display! No art experience is needed—just come, cut out images, and craft your own mini masterpiece. Examples will be available for inspiration.

Sing and Sign Jukebox: Makaton Choir Workshop

The Makaton Choir will teach attendees one song using Makaton, allowing them to join the choir and perform at the end of the session, at approximately 4.30pm.
