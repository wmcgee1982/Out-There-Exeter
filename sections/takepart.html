Call Out

Applications are now open for Exeter Queer Fest, an opportunity for individuals and companies to showcase work or promote society groups that advances the education, awareness and celebration of LGBTQIA+, or promotes inclusivity and the diversity of queer culture.

We're looking for performers, artists, musicians, filmmakers, drag artists, advocates, sportspersons or those who have something totally unique to offer - we want to hear from you!

Venues and Safe Spaces

To make Exeter Queer Fest happen and to offer safe spaces for our LGBTQIA+ artists, networks, society groups, and community, we are working with, and supported by Exeter's flagship culture venues:

  • Barnfield Theatre
  • Exeter Library
  • Exeter Northcott Theatre
  • Exeter Phoenix
  • Royal Albert Memorial Museum
