About Out There Exeter

Out There Exeter was born from the desire for getting out there and celebrating inclusivity and queer culture. We ignite networks, strengthen connections, and celebrate inclusivity within our vibrant LGBTQIA+ community. We are reimagining Exeter's queer landscape by empowering voices out there, to come forward and tell their stories.

Igniting Networks for LGBTQI+ people to connect and build a powerful force for equality and social inclusion.

Strengthening Communities means building safe spaces and fostering connections that uplift and empower each other.

Celebrating Inclusivity welcomes all to be their authentic selves, to belong, and to shine brightly.

Out There Queer Fest

Out There Exeter are excited to deliver a two-week festival of queer culture, an explosion of creative arts, community groups, and advocacy for LGBTQIA+ people and friends. We envision Out There Queer Fest as an annual national event, making Exeter a cultural hub.

Out There Queer Fest, fostering a spirit of inclusion and empowerment, takes place 23rd September to 6th October. Expect a dynamic programme featuring creative expression through artistic performances, cultural events, and community activities. The festival also highlights advocacy efforts, providing a platform to champion equality and challenge discrimination.

We are building on Exeter's strong queer community, society groups, and arts scene. This year's festival and events are supported by core Exeter's flagship cultural venues: Exeter Phoenix, Exeter Northcott Theatre, Barnfield Theatre, Royal Albert Memorial Museum, and Exeter Library.


Queer Culture

Out There Exeter recognise the significance of queer culture to the LGBTQIA+ community. Out There Queer Fest exists to celebrate our culture and to challenge the status quo by making queer culture more accessible and equitable.

Queer culture encompasses the diverse and vibrant experiences of LGBTQIA+ individuals. It's a tapestry woven from threads of self-expression, creativity, community, activism, and resilience.
