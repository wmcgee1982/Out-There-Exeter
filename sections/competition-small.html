Queer Fest: Rising Star
20th September - 6th October


Rising Star Competition

Are you Exeter's next rising star?

If you're 18 or younger, and a solo artist, duo, or group with an original song, we want to hear from you.

Out There Exeter is offering you the chance to close out the Out There Queer Fest, a brand-new, two-week-long arts and culture fringe festival in Exeter, for LGBTQIA+ communities and friends.

Win a chance to:

  • Support Stevie Nicole Brown
  • Perform at the official closing of Out There Queer Fest
  • Perform at Exeter Phoenix's 'New Band Showcase'

Stevie Nicole Brown will select five songs to be shortlisted, which will then be put to a public vote on our social media.

The winner will perform alongside Stevie Nicole Brown at the official closing event of Out There Queer Fest, held at Exeter Phoenix on Sunday, October 6th. Additionally, the winner will have the chance to play a set at one of Exeter Phoenix's 'New Band Showcase' events.

How to enter:

  • Complete the online entry form
  • Submit a video link of you, your duo, or your band performing a song that is at least two minutes long
  • Deadline: Wednesday, 25th September 2024 at 2300 hrs

Make sure your post is set to Public; otherwise, our judges won't be able to see it, and your entry won't be considered.

If you're shortlisted, you'll need to submit a 2-3 minute video to be posted on our social media.
