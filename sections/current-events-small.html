Our events page is a hub for queer-friendly events, venues, and community groups in Exeter. Out There Queer Fest provided an important space for visibility, and we're committed to offer our continued support to the community by helping people connect and celebrate queer arts and culture. If you're organising an event, running a venue, or leading a community group and would like our support in promoting it, drop us an email at info@out-there-exeter.com. We'd be thrilled to promote it on our events page, newsletter, and socials!


We're sorry, but we don't have accessibility information for venues or events we don't host. Please contact the venue or event organisers directly for details.

The Eddystone Trust

The Eddystone Trust enable people to engage in an open discussion about sexual health in the community. You can contact them on 0800 3283508 or by email info@eddystone.org

Website

Proud2Be

Proud2Be is a grassroots, queer-led social enterprise that supports and enables LGBTQIA+ people in Devon and beyond to be free, safe, and proud to be ourselves. You can contact Proud2Be by phone 0333 772 1187 or emailhello@proud2be.org.uk

Website

University of Exeter LGBTQ+ Society

We are a student-led society at the University of Exeter, committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment for all LGBTQ+ students. Our mission is to provide a safe space where everyone can freely express their sexual orientation and/or gender identity without fear of intimidation, discrimination, or harassment.

Website

Trans and Non-binary Café

At the Trans and Non-binary Café we offer a chance to come together with other trans people and discuss whatever you would like. Usually at some point being trans comes up but we believe there’s great value in being with other trans people, having a chance to catch up and talk and make friends with people who share the same sorts of experiences.

Website
