Reach Out Presents: Accessibility for Learning Disabled, Physically Disabled & Neurodivergent LGBTQ+ Pride

May 10 | 2.30 PM

Exeter Phoenix | Family-Friendly

A safe, inclusive space with activities, performances, and a Sing & Sign choir! Email Samuel Pritchard for more details samuel@reachoutdevon.org

About the Event

Reach Out is proud to present an event focused on accessibility and celebration for LGBTQ+ individuals who are learning disabled, physically disabled, or neurodivergent. This is a family-friendly event that prioritises safety, comfort, and inclusivity. Through thoughtful programming and an emphasis on accessibility, we invite everyone to come together and celebrate Pride in a space designed for all bodies and minds.
