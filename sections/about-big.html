We ignite networks, strengthen connections, and celebrate inclusivity within our vibrant LGBTQIA+ community. We are reimagining Exeter's queer landscape by empowering voices out there, to come forward and tell their stories.

Out There Queer Fest

In September 2024, Out There Exeter was excited to deliver a 17-day festival of queer culture, an explosion of creative arts, community groups, and advocacy for LGBTQIA+ people and friends. We envision Out There Queer Fest as an annual national event, making Exeter a cultural hub.

Out There Queer Fest, fostered a spirit of inclusion and empowerment, which took place from 23rd September to 6th October 2024. We delivered a dynamic programme of 40+ events featuring creative expression through artistic performances, cultural events, and community activities. The festival brought together national and local emerging talents. It amplified queer voices, highlighted advocacy efforts, and provided a platform to champion equality and challenge discrimination.


We are proud to celebrate and build upon Exeter's vibrant queer community, dynamic society groups, and thriving arts scene. This year's festival was made possible with the support of Exeter's flagship cultural venues: Exeter Phoenix, Exeter Northcott Theatre, Barnfield Theatre, the Royal Albert Memorial Museum, and Exeter Library. We are grateful to our media partner, Radio Exe, as well as the incredible support from community groups and organisations Exeter City of Literature, Intercom Trust, and Exeter Pride. A special thank you goes to Hotel Indigo Exeter and Telstar Hotel for providing accommodations for our participants and attendees.

Queer Culture

Out There Exeter recognises the significance of queer culture to the LGBTQIA+ community. Out There Queer Fest exists to celebrate our culture and to challenge the status quo by making queer culture more accessible and equitable.


Queer culture encompasses the diverse and vibrant experiences of LGBTQIA+ individuals. It's a tapestry woven from threads of self-expression, creativity, community, activism, and resilience.
