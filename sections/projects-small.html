Our Previous Projects

Out There Exeter is proud to champion creativity, inclusivity, and community through a diverse range of events and pcollaborations. From making Pride accessible, presenting award-winning film screenings to community fundraisers and our flagship Out There Queer Fest, each project highlights our commitment to platforming LGBTQIA+ voices and building connections across Exeter's LGBTQIA+ community. Below showcases our recent projects.


Reach Out Presents: Accessibility for Learning Disabled, Physically Disabled & Neurodivergent LGBTQ+ Pride | May 2025

In partnership with Reach Out, and in collaboration with Exeter Pride, Teignbridge Community Project, Safebreaks Devon CIC, Sing and Sign Jukebox, and Excel–Exeter. An inclusive, fun, and safe space for LGBTQ+ individuals with disabilities and neurodivergence to come together for two and a half hours of fun activities and performances. Whether actively participating or simply enjoying the atmosphere, attendees connected, shared experiences, and expressed themselves freely. Reach Out aims to break down barriers, challenge misconceptions, and ensure greater representation of LGBTQ+ individuals with disabilities, empowering them to engage in social activities and build a stronger, more inclusive community.

Out There Presents: Iris Film Festival | March 2025

A project presented by Out There Exeter in collaboration with the Iris Prize and Exeter Phoenix, showcasing standout films from Iris 2024 that explored love, identity, and the human experience. Sister Wives (15) told the story of two sister wives married to the same man who began to develop feelings for each other, winning the 2024 Iris Prize Best British & Co-op Audience Award. JIA (12A) followed a grieving Chinese mother on a road trip in Australia to remember her late son, where her conservative values were tested when she learned her son’s lover was Eric, winning the 2024 Youth Jury Award. Blood Like Water (15) saw Shadi embark on a secret adventure that forced his family into a difficult decision: collaborate with the Israeli occupation or face humiliation by their own people, winning the 2024 Iris Prize. The Iris Prize is a film and media organisation dedicated to showcasing and promoting LGBTQ+ stories, expanding their reach and impact.

Exeter Pride Fundraiser: Music Bingo | February 2025

We’re thrilled that our Music Bingo fundraiser, in collaboration with Full Moon Exeter, was a huge success—raising just under £500 for Exeter Pride 2025! A massive thank you to everyone who joined us for a night of fun, and to the venue Bomba Exeter for hosting us. Between the Music Bingo and our quiz night we’ve now raised over £700 in total for Exeter Pride.

Out There Queer Fest | October 2025

Out There Exeter proudly delivered a 17-day celebration of queer culture with Out There Queer Fest, a vibrant showcase of over 40 events spanning arts, performance, and community action. The festival championed LGBTQIA+ voices, fostered inclusion, and united national and local talent, supported by key venues like Exeter Phoenix, Northcott Theatre, Barnfield Theatre, RAMM, and Exeter Library. With help from partners including Radio Exe, Exeter City of Literature, Intercom Trust, Exeter Pride, Hotel Indigo, and Telstar Hotel, we proudly celebrated and amplified Exeter’s dynamic queer community.

Exeter Pride Fundraiser: Quiz | September 2025

Supporting the community is foundational to Out There Exeter. Our Out There Queer Fest kicked off with two opening events in collaboration with local community organisers. We proudly supported Climax, Exeter’s only monthly drag night, and teamed up with Full Moon Exeter to host a fundraising quiz night. Both events raised over £200 for Exeter Pride.
