After the success of Out There Exeter in supporting LGBTQIA+ events, venues, and community groups in Exeter, we’re excited to launch a dedicated LGBTQIA+ events page for Plymouth. This events page is a hub for queer-friendly events and spaces, helping to connect communities and celebrate LGBTQIA+ arts, culture, and grassroots organising across the city of Plymouth. If you're organising an event, running a venue, or leading a community group in Plymouth and would like our support in promoting it, drop us an email at info@out-there-exeter.com. We’d love to help share it on our events page, newsletter, and socials!
