28 Feb 24

LGBT+ History Month 2025 Badge Competition

In a display of creativity and advocacy, students at various Exeter schools are taking part in a competition to design the official badge for the 2025 LGBT+ History Month. Held annually throughout February since 2005, this month aims to promote equality and diversity by raising awareness of the experiences and contributions of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender (LGBT+) people.

Exeter City FC Up for Football v Homophobia Award!

Exeter City is proud to be nominated for the prestigious Professional Club Award at the Football v Homophobia Awards! This award recognises clubs that champion LGBTQ+ inclusion in football. The ceremony takes place in Manchester on Friday, February 23rd.
