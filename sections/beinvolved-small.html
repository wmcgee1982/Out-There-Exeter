Be Involved

Registration is Open

Registration is now open for Out There Queer Fest 2024, an opportunity for individuals and organisations to showcase art and culture, or promote society and sports groups that advances the education, awareness and celebration of LGBTQIA+, or promotes inclusivity and the diversity of queer culture.

We're looking for performers, artists, musicians, filmmakers, drag artists, advocates, sportspersons or those who have something totally unique to offer - we want to hear from you!

Participation

Why Take Part

Out There Queer Fest nurtures a less competitive and more affordable environment than other fringe festivals. We offer exposure, networking, collaboration, and career development in a fun and enriching environment, allowing for a valuable experience.

Societies, artists, and advocates can promote work and connect with a welcoming and diverse audience through activities, talks, workshops, performance, and socialising.

Safe Venues and Spaces

To make Out There Queer Fest happen and to offer safe spaces for our LGBTQIA+ artists, networks, society groups, and community, we are working with, and supported by Exeter's flagship cultural venues:

  • Barnfield Theatre
  • Exeter Library
  • Exeter Northcott Theatre
  • Exeter Phoenix
  • Royal Albert Memorial Museum

Who Should Participate

Registration is open for societies, artists, and advocates to participate in Out There Queer Fest 2024. Successful registrations will be included in the online program. Your work should, either:

  • Amplify LGBTQIA+ voices
  • Empower the LGBTQIA+ community
  • Champion LGBTQIA+ well-being
  • Enrich LGBTQIA+ culture

Apply Now

We strive to make the Out There Queer Fest registration process smooth, but we understand it might seem lengthy. If you encounter any difficulties, please don't hesitate to contact us for assistance.

Before you register, consider the following details:

  • Venue Support: Do you need help finding a venue for your event?
  • Ticketing: Will your event require ticketing?
  • Ticket Sales or Participation Fee: Are you interested in collecting revenue through ticket sales, or would you prefer a flat participation fee?
