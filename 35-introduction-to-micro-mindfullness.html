An Introduction to Micro-Mindfulness

Sun, 06 Oct 2024 | Royal Albert Memorial Museum (RAMM)

Event Details

Date: Sun, 06 Oct 2024

Time: 13:00 PM - 14:00 PM

Running Time: 1 hour

Venue: Royal Albert memorial Museum Art & Gallery, Queen Street, Exeter,EX4 3RX

Genre: Wellbeing, Retreat, Health

Event Description

Out There Queer Fest: An Introduction to Micro-Mindfulness


As Out There Queer Fest draws to a close after more than two weeks of exciting events, take a moment to unwind and recharge with "An Introduction to Micro-Mindfulness.


In our fast-paced world, finding time for self-care can be challenging, but mindfulness doesn't have to be time-consuming to be effective. Join us for "An Introduction to Micro-Mindfulness," a practical workshop that will teach you how to integrate mindfulness into your daily life, even when time is short.


Discover how to cultivate calm and presence in just a few moments each day, and leave with tools you can use anytime, anywhere.


Designed for LGBTQ+ individuals and friends, this session offers simple yet powerful techniques that fit into even the tightest schedules. Come relax, learn, and connect with your community as you discover how to find calm amidst the chaos.
