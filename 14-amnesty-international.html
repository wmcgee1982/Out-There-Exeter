Exeter Amnesty International's Annual Walk

Sun, 29 Sept 2024 | Bromhams Farm car park | FREE

Event Details

Date: Sun, 29 Sept 2024

Time: 10.30 AM

Venue: Bromhams Farm Car Park, EX2 8QE

Genre: Walk, Human Rights


This is our group's main fundraising event of the year and voluntary donations can be made by walkers and supporters using cash or card on the day, or via our JustGiving page.


Amnesty Exeter Instagram


Event Description

Meet at Bromhams Farm car park, EX2 8QE, from 10.30 for a 10.45 start. As it is a Sunday, free parking should be available in Marsh Barton, or there is a train from Exmouth via Exeter stations arriving at the new Marsh Barton station at 10.35.


The walk will be about 5-7 miles and is flat along the river valley with mostly gravel or tarmac paths. There will be options to return to the start earlier for anyone who does not want to walk the whole route.


Dogs (on leads when necessary) and children are welcome. Toilets are available when we pass the quay.


Please bring snacks and drinks. When we have finished, there will be an opportunity to picnic and to sign letters for Amnesty prisoners of conscience.
