Event Description

“UK Hun?!” Get Ready for Our First “Rodeo”
Welcome to Out There Queer Fest's unapologetically exclusive 'Extravaganza' club night! Join the fierce and fabulous Bimini, the sensational star of RuPaul's Drag Race UK, renowned queer artist, author, and icon.


Turning up the heat are raw, wyld and UNTAMED Burlesque, the boldest burlesque and cabaret stars from the South West and beyond. Untamed Burlesque champion LGBTQIA+ performers from other marginalised communities. Expected sexy, scary, silly and damn right weird! Featuring:

  • Mx Cyanide: Intertnational draglesque star
  • Merlin The Grey: Boylesque badass
  • Violent May: The all round weirdo
  • Ally Way: Camp clownery and silliness
  • Bea Sweet: Neo-burlesque sweetpie

Not to mention 3 dancefloors with at least 6 DJs!


More announcements soon....


Category is “Extravaganza” Come dressed in your most glamorous, bold, and ostentatious attire, ready to strut your stuff and take part in our Pose-style Masquerade. The winner takes it all - our best dressed will be the recipient of our coveted prize!


Our club night at Out There Queer Fest promises to be a landmark underground event of queer uniqueness and edgy renaissance. Don't miss out on the queerest and fiercest night of your life!
