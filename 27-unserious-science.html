(Un)Serious Science: PRISM Exeter and University of Exeter

Sat, 05 Oct 2024 | Exeter Library

Event Details

Date: Sat, 05 Oct 2024

Time: 02:00 PM - 04:30 PM

Running Time: 2 hours 30 mins

Venue: Exeter Library, Castle Street, Exeter, EX4 3PQ

Genre: STEM, Workshop, Seminar, Discussion, Education

Event Description

(Un)Serious Science is an event hosted by PRISM Exeter and the University of Exeter.


Come and enjoy putting the fun back in the fundamentals of science, technology, engineering, and maths (STEM), and discover your identity as a LGBTQ+ scientist!


The talks and workshop will be conducted by LGBTQ+ STEM professionals/students from the local community. Part of the workshops will also highlight PRISM's recent Queer Science Competition, in which young people around the South West nominated an LGBTQ+ scientist they found inspirational.
