Heartstopper

Sun, 06 Oct 2024 | Exeter Library | FREE

Event Details

Date: Sun, 06 Oct 2024

Time: 12:00 PM

Venue: Exeter Library, Castle Street, Exeter, EX4 3PQ

Genre: Young Person's Day, Gaming, Workshops, Open Mic, Guest Speakers

Age Guidance: Under 18's

Out There Queer Fest Instagram


Event Description

Join us for an exciting Heartstopper-themed event celebrating the beloved graphic novel and Netflix series! Enjoy guest speakers. Unleash your creativity in our comic book and anime creation workshop. Share your story and engage in discussions on themes like sexuality, friendship, love, mental health, bullying. Or simply come and hang with like minded people and to young people in a safe space.

  • Open Mic Stage: Come and play an instrument, song or perform
  • Anime and Comic Book: Workshop by professionals
  • Gaming Area: Relax and play PlayStation/Switch
  • Breakout Space: A private area for confidential conversations with a young peer
  • Stalls and Refreshments: Enjoy relevant stalls and refreshments in the reception area
  • Themed Book Display: Explore a curated selection of comics and LGBTQIA+ themed books

Don't miss this opportunity to connect, create, and celebrate!


The Heartstopper-themed event amplifies LGBTQIA+ voices by providing an open mic for attendees to share their stories, empowers the LGBTQIA+ community through creative workshops and themed displays that validate their experiences, champions LGBTQIA+ well-being by addressing important themes like mental health and providing a safe space for private conversations, and enriches LGBTQIA+ culture with diverse activities, including screenings, presentations, and social spaces that celebrate and connect the community.


This event is in partnership with Exeter City of Literature
