Out There Queer Fest 2024

Out There Queer Fest, proudly presented by Out There Exeter, took place from September 20th to October 6th, 2024, celebrating queer culture, creativity, and community. Bringing together national and local talents, the festival showcased an eclectic mix of performances, workshops, and events, spanning drag, cabaret, theatre, and more. Designed to amplify queer voices and provide a platform for emerging artists, Out There Queer Fest fostered connection, inclusivity, and joy. From bold performances to thought-provoking discussions and unforgettable club nights, the festival was a true celebration of self-expression and pride in all its forms.

Out There Queer Fest

Extravaganza Club Night

Queer Fest's unapologetically 'Extravaganza' club night headlined by the fierce Bimini, with host Arina Fox , and performances by Untamed Burlesque, and the electrifying Haus of Calamari Girls. Featuring 6 DJ's across two dancefloors!


Supported by Exeter Phoenix
