Event Description

A mixed bill cabaret, drag and music hall show, with lipsynch, live music, burlesque and more!


Willkommen, bienvenue, ELLO MAATES!


Welcome to RiffRaff Kabarett - a disreputable den of decadence and debauchery (that won't break the bank!). Inspired by the infamous Weimar Republic, RiffRaff Kabarett is serving you a dark slice of 1930s Berlin mixed with a healthy kick of contemporary, working class political punkery. So dust off your glad rags for a devious and downright filthy night of music, games, burlesque, drag, and anything in between!


Through cabaret, RiffRaff explores the historical parallels between today and the Weimar Republic period, which saw a boom in the arts, culture and queer rights. Hosted by resident trashbags Ruby Wednesday & Molly Beth Morossa, with all star special guests to be announced!


There's no dress code, but why not get glammed up, channel your inner Marlene Dietrich, or Emcee or express yourself in your favourite get up!


The show is Weimar Era inspired, so we draw comparisons between this period in history and current events. We may make reference to Nazi Germany, WW1 & WW2, and current political parties!


"unexpected, joyful and gleefully subversive" - Rockshot Magazine


"provocative, unexpected and artistically disruptive in the most exciting way." - Rockshot Magazine
