Event Description

Dir. Howard Deutsch

1986 | 96 mins | US

Molly Ringwald, Jon Cryer, Andrew McCarthy


Pretty in Pink (Back to School Special)


As voted for by Dolly's Film Club at Election, we are bringing you the 80s classic Pretty in Pink!


This brat pack classic from the John Hughes universe follows Molly Ringwald as Andie, highschool outsider, friend of the (iconic) Duckie, she works at a record store and aspire for more in life.


A classic love triangle story, with tangles of social status, economic class and THAT DRESS.


Join Dolly for Pretty in Pink as our second annual Back to School September screening!


Cat Nip

As always with Dolly's Film Club there's a little extra catnip, for Pretty in Pink prizes will be available for the worst prom dresses in attendance!


Dolly's Film Club

Dolly's Film Club is a monthly film club that celebrates the camp, fun, queer, under-appreciated and unapologetically enjoyable side of cinema, but most of all Dolly's Film Club is to celebrate the JOY of being an audience.


You are always encouraged to wear fancy dress or 'vibes' outfits to Dolly's Film Club, and there's always a little bit extra catnip involved in our screenings.


Everyone is welcome, come as a group or by yourself, as soon as you come to a Dolly's Film Club event you are in our club!
