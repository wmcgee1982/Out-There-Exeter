Event Description

Prepare yourself to witness the Art of Drag like never before!


Virina Flower is presenting you with the extravagant, show-stopping and top-quality drag of Three different Nations.


For your viewing pleasure, we are bringing to the stage the Queen of Wales and the absolute star Polly Amorous, the Ukrainian Queen of the scene and your host for the night Virina Flower and the grand diva herself representing Poland, Drag Race UK superstar Alexis Saint Pete.


We're here to deliver jaw-dropping pure magic performances you'll never forget, as well as a more intimate and open Q&A segment in which the audience will get the chance to participate.


Expect high energy and vulnerable moments, Glits & Glam, laughter and tears.
